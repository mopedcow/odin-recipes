Tuna Pasta Salad

Description

Another easy to make recipe, good for lunch or a lazy dinner. Creamy mayo combines with tuna and pasta to make deicious concoction. Add veg for crunch and health!

Ingredients

  • 2 tins tuna, drained
  • 2 or 3 heaping Tbsp mayonnaise
  • Enough pasta for 2 people. I use the spiral tubes but you can use whatever shape you like
  • 1 cucumber, chopped
  • 2 or 3 sprigs scallions, chorped
  • 1 brocolli crown, chopped
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • Paprika could be nice? (optional)

Method

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package
  2. Meanwhile, scoop the tuna into a small bowl and add the mayo. You can adjust the amount of mayo to your own taste
  3. Also add the veg, salt and pepper, and whatever other herbs you think will taste good. Now stir to combine
  4. Once the pasta has cooked, drain and rinse with cold water. Replace the pasta back into the pan or other medium-sized bowl
  5. Add the tuna-mayo mixture and stir to combine
  6. Place inside your picnic basket. Go to the park. Eat your lovely lunch, greedily
