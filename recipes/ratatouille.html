Ratatouille

Description

An easy to make version of a delicious French dish. Aubergine, courgette, onion, garlic and tomato are first saut√©ed in oil then combined, with the addition of basil and/or oregano. I like to add tomato sauce and pour it on top of spaghetti. It's good!! This recipe should make enough for 2 hungry people with a bit left over for lunch tomorrow.

Ingredients

  • 2 Courgettes, chopped big
  • 1 Aubergine, chopped big
  • 1 medium brown onion, chopped
  • 3-4 small or 2 big tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 jar tomato passata
  • Lots of garlic, minced
  • Oil for frying
  • 2-ish Tbsp Basil
  • 1/2 Tbsp Oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Enough spaghetti for 2

Method

  1. Fry the aubergine until it's browned. The pieces should be firm--no need to cook thouroughly yet. Remove from pan, then do the same with the courgette. Remove the courgette.
  2. Fry the garlic and onion until it's soft. Then add the aubergine and courgette back to the pan
  3. Add the tomatoes, passata, basil, oregano, and salt and pepper. Cook thouroughly until hot and the veg are soft.
  4. I forgot to tell you to cook the pasta. Do that.
  5. Serve the saucy ratatouille over the pasta, top with parmesan.
  6. PS you could also add bell peppers if you want. Chop and cook the same way as the aubergine and courgette.
